ZAZ Studio E-Decor Package

Our e-decor digital design package is a simplified, more affordable way for you to get the designer space that you are looking for without the limitations of location and long-term commitments. Each e-decor package is personalized to be suited to your unique style, space, budget, and timeline.

How it Works

Once you contact us requesting our e-decor services, we send you a questionnaire for you to complete - this will give us some insight as to your preferred style and ideal functionality of the space. We also need you to send us pictures and measurements of the room(s). Once we establish the budget and direction of the design, we create a thorough Digital Design Package which includes the following:


Room Concept Board: includes a visual collage of all materials and furniture used in the space that will allow you to see all the components come together to fit your ideal style for the space.


Space Planning: includes a detailed furniture layout plan, drawn to scale. This plan will include all selected furniture to ensure that everything we propose fits comfortably in your space.


Rendered Views & Sketches: these 3D views and sketches will provide a clear visual understanding of how the space will look once it is completed.


Shopping list: these are thorough specifications of each item proposed for the space, including dimensions, website links, vendors, manufacturers, and their contact information. We want you to be completely ecstatic with the final result, so to ensure your satisfaction, we also provide 3 iterations of this package proposal.
