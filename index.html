ABOUT

I started my career as a prop master's assistant, working on sets for Broadway and Off-Broadway shows. The experience ignited my passion for spatial design. Since then, I have worked as an interior designer for a variety of well-known firms developing designs for hotels, restaurants, and residential homes. For over half a decade I've cultivated my technique as a spatial designer, growing in the profession. It is my belief that events and environments should evoke emotions and design should have narrative. I also believe that budget guides a project and strategic planning must be implemented in order to ensure extraordinay results. I started ZAZ Studio because I want to create memorable immersive experiences through creative planning and efficient management at the right cost for you. Here, we turn your ideas into actions, dreams into realities, concepts into celebrations!

Flora Kogan, Owner & Lead Designer florakogan.com
